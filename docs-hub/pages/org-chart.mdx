## How It Works

1. **You give direction** — natural language, any complexity
2. **Chief of Staff triages** — classifies intent, routes to the right VP
3. **VP plans** — breaks down into tasks, assigns to specialists
4. **Agents execute** — each specialist works their domain autonomously
5. **Results roll up** — Chief of Staff synthesizes and reports back

Every agent has access to encrypted databases, knowledge bases, and learns from past outcomes via the ML system. The organization improves over time.
